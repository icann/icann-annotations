#C RFC 3007 adds DNSSEC to UPDATE; RFCs 4033, 4034, and 4035 don't
#L 1
<p>
@@RFC3007@@ describes how DNSSEC (the version used around 2000)
can be used with the protocol described in RFC 2136.
</p>
<p>
@@RFC4033@@ explicitly says "The mechanisms outlined above are not designed to
protect operations such as zone transfers and dynamic update
([RFC2136], [RFC3007])."
</p>
